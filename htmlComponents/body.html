Perfect Pancakes

10 Minute Prep Time

4 Servings

250 Calories

By Tim Reardon

chobani.com

Get the family out of bed with the smell of pancakes on the griddle. This delicious Perfect Pancake recipe is an excellent twist on a timeless classic. Adding Chobani Greek Yogurt makes pancakes fluffier, while also providing the added benefit of extra protein. The cultured milk in Chobani brings out a delicious tartness comparable to buttermilk, but with healthier ingredients. Whether topped with fresh fruit, real maple syrup, or even another scoop of Chobani, your Saturday morning breakfast just got better.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Plain Chobani Whole Milk Greek Yogurt
  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • Maple syrup and berries, for serving

Preparation

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. In a small bowl, whisk together egg, Chobani and milk.
  2. Add yogurt mixture to flour mixture and use a wooden spoon to combine lightly
  3. Heat a nonstick skilled over medium heat. Lightly coat with cooking spray and pour about Â¼ cup of batter onto pan for each pancake. Cook until edges set and bubbles appear on surface, about 2 minutes. Flip to brown other side.
  4. Transfer to a large plate and repeat with remaining batter. Serve with maple syrup and berries.
